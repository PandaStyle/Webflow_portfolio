About

Hi! I'm Zsolt, currently Budapest based software engineer.

Throughout my professional career I've been always chasing the limits what the web platform is capable of, let it be performance, UX, frameworks, accessibility, testing, monitoring and so on.

What drives me most in my career is to be able to solve impactful, challenging customer problems by leveraging state of the art technology, UI and innovation.

As a web developer my goal is to always present the user a pleasing browsing experience via a well-designed, fast and performant  interface. I-m a form believer of the importance of first impression, and that's true for the web as well. In awe with the forever evolving web ecosystem and always looking to find a way to try out new and better technologies.
Especially a big fan of webGL visualizations.

Throughout my career I've been lucky enough to work as a web dev for one of the few Hungarian unicorns called LogMeIn: both in Budapest and in Boston, MA. After that I've joined Truemtion where I've become a team lead and engineering manager.

When I'm not tinkering with bits and bytes I'm an extremely passionate cyclist (road, xc, gravel you name it) and snowboarder. I'm also a certified woodworker, mostly working on fun projects for my family and friends.
